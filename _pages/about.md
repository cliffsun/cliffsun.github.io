I study physics, math, and computer Science at the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/).

Broadly speaking, I'm interested in the synergy of quantum hardware and algorithms towards building a quantum computer. I've published [3 first-author papers](publications.md) and filed a patent on the applications of one-dimensional superconductivity in classical and quantum logic. One of those papers solved a 30 year problem. 

I also hold a joint research position at the [Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory](https://www.jhuapl.edu/), where I investigate classical machine learning applications to learn quantum compilation schemes. 

You can reach me at cliffxs2@illinois.edu.
